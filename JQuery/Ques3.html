    About

    Established in 2014, Woxsen University is the flag-bearer of the first Private Universities of the young state of Telangana. It is ranked 4th among B-Schools in India and also ranked 14th among the Top 100 B-Schools Private Category, courtesy of IIRF 2022, Education Post. The university has made its debut on India’s Best Business Schools list (beyond IIMs) by Dalal Street Investment Journal. Woxsen University, reckoned as one of the Top universities in Hyderabad, is formed by 4 constituent schools – Woxsen School of Business (WSB), Woxsen School of Technology, Woxsen School of Arts and Science and Woxsen School of Architecture & Planning. It offers undergraduate programs in BBA, BBA+MBA, BA (Hons.), B.Com (Hons.), B.Tech, B.Des (Hons.), B.Arch. & Postgraduate programs in MBA, MBA (Financial Services), MBA (Business Analytics) and Ph.D programs. The University has started two new programs BA-LLB and BBA-LLB in the school of law. Woxsen University provides 100% placement assistance to its students 124 companies visited for on-campus recruitment. The highest package is recorded at INR 16 LPA. The university offers undergraduate and postgraduate programs in the discipline of Management, Arts & Design, Engineering, Architecture & Planning, Liberal Arts & Humanities.
